Today we are making a delicious Breakfast Sandwhich

Living alone and looking for a great way to start your day? How about something quick and easy to make? A breakfast sandwhich is a great choice to solve all these problems with a total cook time of just 10 min!

By: Darryl Henry

Ingredients:

4 slices of bread
3 Eggs
3 tsp. butter
Bacon/Sausage
Cheese
Onion optional
Peppers optional
Vegtible Oil
Mustard optional

What to do:

Begin with taking two slices of bread and toasting them.

Next take the olive oil and greese the entire pan.

Begin to scramble two or 3 eggs in the pan.

Chop the onions and peppers and add it to the pan

Take a separate pan and begin to fry the bacon/sausage

Complete the sandwhich by taking all cook items and putting them together.
